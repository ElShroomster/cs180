Project 0 - Becoming Friends with Your Camera

The first step towards knowing every pixel is to become best friends with your camera, especially the one in your smartphone. In particular, the goal of this project is to get you some intuitive understanding of the somewhat subtle relationship between perspective, focal length and zoom, and the center of projection.
